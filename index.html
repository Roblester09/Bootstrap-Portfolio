About

My name is Robert. I enjoy creating websites!

I currently live in Orlando,FL. My absolute favorite thing to do is to try new restaurants or bars around the area. There is never a shortage of new places to visit.

I enjoy creating websites and games from the ground up. I start with mocking up a desing in either Sketch or Photoshop. Then I begin with coding out the site from top to bottom. Sometimes the final product turns into something a little different than the mock-up only because I believe an idea evolves as it becomes a reality.

I favor Front End Development because I'm a visual enthusiast when it comes to creating something. I enjoy seeing the project unfold as I develop it. I do have the mindset of a Full Stack Developer in that my code is easily inbedded with server side logic to make the Back End merger more seamless.

Go ahead and check out some of my recent work below! If you enjoy it then don't hesitate to let me know by sending over an email.
